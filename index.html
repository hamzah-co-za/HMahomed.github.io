About Me

I began my journey by building and maintaining the IT infrastructure at my high school by deploying Microsoft products, optimizing systems, and ensuring a reliable digital learning environment. This early, hands-on experience taught me how technology directly empowers productivity and education, forming the foundation of my technical and problem-solving mindset.

Since then, I’ve expanded my expertise through globally recognized certifications from AWS, Google, and IBM. I have gathered a vast amount of skills in AI engineering, cloud architecture, cybersecurity, business intelligence, and automation. These certifications didn’t just build knowledge; they inspired practical applications all the way from cloud deployments and AI app prototypes to workflow automation and data analysis projects.

My focus now is on transforming these skills into real-world impact by designing secure, intelligent, and scalable solutions that solve business problems, enhance efficiency, and align technology with business strategies. Whether it’s through system implementation, AI-driven insights, or cross-functional collaboration, I aim to create solutions that drive measurable value.
